The Cryptopedia is a community encyclopedia about crypto-currencies, crypto-economy and the blockchain technology. When we first get started with cryptocurrencies, terms and concepts may sound foreign. It can take significant time to understand how to implement the information and dive into the amazing possibilities available in digital investments and decentralized finances. The goal of The Cryptopedia is to help you get information about basic concepts, faster and in a friendly manner. The Cryptopedia is free to use, and all are welcome to join in contributing and building the archives.
