The Cryptopedia is a community encyclopedia about crypto-currencies, crypto-economy and the blockchain technology. When I was getting started with crypto, every term and concept was foreign to me. So, it took me significant time to understand how to implement the information and dive into the possibilities available in decentralized finances. That's why I created The Cryptopedia: to help you get educated and informed faster and in a friendly manner. The Cryptopedia is free to use, and all are welcome to join in building the archives as a community. Sign up to create your account and start learning and contributing today!
